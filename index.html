Multi-Track Therapy

Welcome to the Tinnitus Synthesizer. Instead of just one sound, you can now layer multiple sounds to match your exact tinnitus profile.

How to use:

  1. Add Layers: Click "+ Add Sound Layer" to add a Tone or a Noise.
  2. Sculpt: Adjust the Frequency (Pitch) and Volume for each layer individually.
  3. Test: Click the "Speaker" icon on each layer to hear it. Layer them until they sound like your "Ghost".
  4. Start Therapy: The app will take your custom mixture and pulse it in a random pattern to desynchronize your brain.
